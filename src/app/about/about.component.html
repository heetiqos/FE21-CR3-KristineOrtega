About Us


We started as a small business, envisioning what a perfumery shop could become: providing excellent service, engaging with customers, offering quality products, and affordable prices.
In March 2021, we launched our first location near the city center of Vienna. Our operation was clear; we began with two salespersons and focused on maintaining good relationships with our suppliers. As our customer base expanded and our shop grew, we ventured into online selling. Today, with over a million active customers, Aromatica is one of the leading online perfume shops in Austria. We proudly offer 500 national and international brands, with over 10,000 products, providing a large selection to cater to everyone's preferences.
